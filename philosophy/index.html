This audio essay presents The Managed Self, an extended analysis of the intersection between Christopher Lasch’s critique of the narcissistic personality and Günther Anders’ philosophy of technological modernity. The essay argues that contemporary narcissism is not merely a moral failure but a structural adaptation to a civilization increasingly governed by technological systems, algorithmic evaluation, and mediated representations of identity.
